How I Recycle

I specialize in smaller items, but I will take anything under 50lbs
I separate the plastics from the metals
I throw plastic in my recycling bin and I sort the metals into different groups because of the recycling plant I take them to
When I have aquired enough of one metal, I then take that metal, and any other metals to the recycling plant
That's basically it, I can go into more detail if you want.
