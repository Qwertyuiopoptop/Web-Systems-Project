Camille Doering, Web Systems

This website is being created for Sue Lemke, regarding a recycling business, and will meet the required objectives for this project as follows:

  • The webiste will have headers, to differentiate between different pages and topics.
  • The site will have at least 10 pages, dedicated to a variety of general topics, and more specific ones.
  • This site will have navigation links to allow users to navigate between different pages.
  • Images will be provided, to allow users a better visual idea of what they are looking at, for pages such as one which lists different types of recyclable objects.
  • Files will be clearly named, in lowercase, and well-organized.
  • There will be at least one external link, maybe to something such as govt. sites detailing certain recycling based acts.
  • Source code will be well formated, with logical and meaningful class and ID names.
  • Header, footer, and navbar will be included.
  • Users will have a way to log in, and their information may be saved using a database, maybe SQL?

My groupmates and I have a rough idea of what the website will look like, and have discussed this with each other.
